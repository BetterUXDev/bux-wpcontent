About betterux.design

This site is a hub for user experience advocates across the world to connect and collaborate. Through case studies focused on UX, we hope to create a space where you can share your thoughts and opinions on current trends.

Meet the Team

The betterUX.design team is composed of a diverse group of creative individuals with a shared passion for User Experience. We are committed to identifying and improving our understanding of UX design. Together, we are building a meaningful community that strives to advance UX design through our shared knowledge, experience and inspiration.

Join the Team

We are always on the look out for UX savvy team members who can help assemble Case Studies, support authors, help us administer the site, and help reach out to our growing community. Become a member!
